Cancellation Policy

Overview

We understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both our guests and our business. Please read the following terms carefully before making a booking.

Refund Structure

Cancellation Time Refund Amount Notes
30+ days before 95% 5% processing fee
15-29 days before 90% 10% processing fee
7-14 days before 50% 50% cancellation fee
Less than 7 days 0% No refund
No-show 0% 100% charge applies

Special Circumstances

Weather Conditions

In case of severe weather conditions making travel unsafe, we will offer to reschedule your tour or provide a full refund at no extra charge.

Government Restrictions

If travel to Kashmir is restricted by government orders, a full refund will be issued or your booking can be rescheduled without penalty.

Medical Emergencies

For cancellations due to medical emergencies (with valid documentation), we offer flexible rescheduling or partial refunds on a case-by-case basis.

How to Cancel

To cancel your booking, please contact us immediately:

  • Email: travelkashmir11@gmail.com
  • Phone: +91 8899434467 (24/7)
  • WhatsApp: +91 8899434467

Please include your booking reference number in all cancellation communications. Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method.

Rescheduling

You can reschedule your tour once without any penalty if done at least 7 days before the original travel date. Rescheduling requests made less than 7 days before may incur a 20% fee.

Partial Cancellations

If your group size reduces after booking, refunds for the canceled travelers will follow the same refund structure above. Additional charges may apply if the group size falls below the minimum required for group rates.
