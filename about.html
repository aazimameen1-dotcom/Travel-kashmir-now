Our Story

Travel Kashmir Now was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate local Kashmiris who wanted to share the true beauty of their homeland with the world. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted tour operators in Kashmir.

We believe in responsible tourism that benefits local communities while providing visitors with authentic, unforgettable experiences. Our deep local knowledge and connections allow us to show you Kashmir's hidden gems that most tourists never discover.
